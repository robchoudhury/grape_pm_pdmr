This folder contains data related to a grape powdery mildew severity trial conducted in Round Mountain CA in 2011 and 2012. Parts of this data were published as a Plant Disease Management Report (PDMR) https://www.plantmanagementnetwork.org/pub/trial/pdmr/reports/2015/SMF018.pdf
The co-authors are currently re-submitting the rest of the data  for the 2012 year.

This trial was conducted in conjunction between Doug Gubler (Extension Specialist), Dan Marcum (Extension Agent) and Hans Heinrich (grower). We thank Mr Hans Heinrich for lending us part of his vineyard to run our trial. 

Data from this study is copyrighted to the University of California Davis, unauthorized use is strictly prohibited.
